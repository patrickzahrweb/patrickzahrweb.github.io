PORTRAIT

Daring contrasts and different textures all while finding a certain homogeneity, revisiting the classics, crossing the eras. For his creations, Patrick Zahr diverts the established codes with an ingenuous spirit and a lot of lightness. He therefore marks his chic and spontaneous style with a modern elegance and a natural fluidity. Through his creations, the body is structured by graphic lines, sensuality is suggested by movement. In a country filled with contrasts and elegance Lebanon, Patrick was born and raised in a small millennial Phoenician town on the Mediterranean coast, a short distance from Bayreuth. From a young age, Patrick is fascinated and attracted to textures and colors, by meticulous craftsmanship. He marked his professors by his drawings and his creativity. In Bayreuth, he started his career as an interior designer where he constantly searched to recreate a unique universe for each of his clients to feel good, from Bayreuth to Montreal. Patrick work little for others, self-taught, he prefers to learn by himself through his experiences. He now expresses himself by fashion design through what makes him feel alive and animated: far from the established codes, he creates his own universe with freedom and natural and fresh modernism, at the service of women and men that he meets and that he loves to magnify. Patrick Zahr creates his own label under his own name, a hybrid identity where the oriental softness and beauty irrigate a western style and brings him a refreshing breeze of the Orient. His workshop elaborates from noble fabrics and fabrics with ethnic character with modern and precise silhouettes all at once, like a heritage of his training as an interior architectural designer that marks his comprehension of fashion. His influences are a mix of his Lebanese origins, his adoptive Canada and his numerous trips to Paris, a source of so many inspirations and breaths.
